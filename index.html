Why Classroom Libraries?

In many rural communities, children lack access to books and resources that encourage reading. Classroom libraries bring learning directly into classrooms, bridging gaps in literacy and creating opportunities for self-learning and curiosity.

Our initiative helps students build confidence and communication skills through engaging, age-appropriate books in English and Telugu.
